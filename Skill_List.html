Skills

Close Range

Unarmed ***** Become a kung fu master. Each rank in Unarmed increase attack rolls, damage rolls, and AC to close range (within 5ft) combat by +1.
Melee ***** Go get'em, slugger. Each rank in Melee increase attack rolls and damage rolls with a melee weapons (not unarmed) by +1.

Long Range

Bow ***** Not only do you dress like Legolas but you weld the bow just as well as the Elven Prince. Each rank in Bow increase attack rolls and damage rolls with a sling, bow, or crossbow by +1.
Pistolero ***** You've watch too many westerns. Each rank in Pistolero increase attack rolls and damage rolls with pistols and revolvers by +1. At rank three, reloading a pistol or revolver is now a swift action instead of a move action.
Rifleman ***** This is my rifle... Each rank in Rifleman increase attack rolls and damage rolls with rifles and shotguns by +1. At rank three, reloading a rifle or (pump or mag-feed) shotgun is now a move action instead of a full round action. Also, a swift action to load one round or shell into a bolt action or shotgun now loads two instead.
