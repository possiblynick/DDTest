Big Picture

Learn to code while building out my RPG campign

Campaign plot

PCs will try to escape their location and reach safety from the sudden invasion of ZOMBIES!!!

The game be run like West March where each session will be like a stage in Left 4 Dead.

This will be more helpful for new players that are curious about RPGs but don't want to commit the time.

Things I want in the campaign

  • Inventory Management
  • Gear Durability
  • Item usable amounts (duct tape has 3 uses)
  • Crafting rules (needs 4 uses of duct tape to make a spear)
  • AI Director (if PCs are standing around, a horde can walk in on them)
  • Medical stuff takes time to work (no magic heals)
  • Custom classes (inplace of classes, it will be working jobs and you get bonuses from that)
  • To replace D&D races for benefits, will use PCs background (like did the play sports as a kid)
